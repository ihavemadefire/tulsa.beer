About Tulsa.Beer

Tulsa.Beer was launched in the early spring of 2021 as a protfolio project for the Holberton School front end curriculum.

The hope is that in time with improvements in the code base, a spiffier front end, a whole lot of marketing legwork, this can become a local version of beeradvocate or other breweing geek sites.
